BLOCK Q4 2025 AND FULL-YEAR FINANCIAL RESULTS

Q4 2025 HIGHLIGHTS:
- Revenue: $6.25 billion (+3.65% YoY)
- Gross profit: $2.87 billion (+24% YoY)
- Operating income: $485 million
- Adjusted operating income: $588 million (+46% YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $930 million
- Adjusted diluted EPS: +38% YoY

FULL-YEAR 2025:
- Full-year gross profit: $10.36 billion (+17% YoY)
- Annual revenue: $24.19 billion (+0.30% growth)

CASH APP SEGMENT (Q4 2025):
- Cash App gross profit: $1.83 billion (+33% YoY)
- Monthly actives: 59 million (returned to growth)
- Primary banking actives: 9.3 million (+22% YoY in December)
- Commerce enablement volume: $54.7 billion (+17% YoY)
- Consumer lending originations: +69% YoY in Q4; +50% for full year
- Borrow originations: +223% YoY in Q4
- Cash App Green "personalized offers attach rate grew from 2% to 14%"

SQUARE SEGMENT (Q4 2025):
- GPV growth: 10.3% in Q4; 10% for full year (up from 8.6% in 2024)
- New Volume Added (NVA): 29% YoY in Q4; 17% for full year (record)
- Sales-led NVA: +62% YoY
- Independent sales organizations: Over 100 partners
- Hardware/processing costs: each a 2-percentage-point headwind to gross profit growth

BITCOIN ECOSYSTEM:
- Bitcoin treasury: 8,883 BTC (valued at ~$777.5M at year-end)
- Proto mining rigs: initial shipments contributing to Q4 results
- Square processed $250B GPV from 4.5M+ sellers and 5.9B transactions in 2025
- Cash App handled $316B inflows

CAPITAL RETURNS:
- Q4 share repurchases: $790 million (11.9 million Class A shares)
- 2025 total repurchases: $2.3 billion
- Intent to maintain annually
- $5.3 billion remaining under authorization
- Total liquidity: $9.2 billion

WORKFORCE RESTRUCTURING:
- Workforce reduction: 40%+ (from over 10,000 to under 6,000)
- Expected restructuring charges: $450-$500 million
- Majority of charges in Q1 2026
- Execution substantially complete by end of Q2 2026

2026 GUIDANCE (RAISED):
- Gross profit: $12.2 billion (+18% YoY)
- Q1 gross profit: $2.8 billion (+22% YoY)
- Adjusted operating income: $3.2 billion (+54% growth, 6 points margin expansion)
- Q1 adjusted operating income: $600 million
- Adjusted diluted EPS: $3.66 (+54% growth)
- Q1 adjusted diluted EPS: $0.67 (+920% YoY)
- Net interest expense: Q1 $60M, full-year $200M

AI/PRODUCTIVITY METRICS:
- Jack Dorsey: "Intelligence tools have changed what it means to build and run a company"
- "Production code shipped per engineer increased by over 40% since September"
- Positioning as "a smaller, faster, intelligence-native company"

PRODUCT LAUNCHES MENTIONED:
- Cash App Green: Primary banking program (launched November 2025)
- Square Handheld & Square Register 2.0: New hardware devices
- Afterpay pre-purchase: Launched February 2026
- Pay in 4: BNPL for peer-to-peer (industry first)
- Proto mining rigs: Contributing to Q4

RISK FACTORS NOTED:
- Higher Borrow portfolio losses in early 2026 tied to "record first-time Borrow actives"
