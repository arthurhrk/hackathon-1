BLOCK Q4 2024 EARNINGS CALL — FORWARD-LOOKING COMMITMENTS (Baseline)

3. Q1 2025 GPV growth: high single digits (constant currency, adjusting for leap year)

STRATEGIC COMMITMENTS FOR 2025:

7. Square upmarket expansion
8. Bitcoin mining (Proto) development
