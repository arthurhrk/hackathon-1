FINANCIAL RESULTS — Q3 2025:
- Gross Profit: $2.66 billion (up 18% YoY, accelerating from 14% growth in Q2)
- Adjusted Operating Income: $480 million
- Highest adjusted operating income and adjusted EBITDA on record at that point

CASH APP PERFORMANCE:
- Gross profit grew 24% year-over-year
- 58 million monthly active users in September
- Gross profit per monthly transacting active: $94 (up 25% YoY)
- Cash App Borrow: 34% origination growth with stable risk loss and 24% annualized net margins
- Post-purchase BNPL on Cash App Card: $3 billion in annualized originations by early October
- Improved onboarding, referrals, and payment flows
- Reduced friction while boosting engagement and retention

SQUARE PERFORMANCE:
- Gross profit grew 9% year-over-year
- GPV grew 12%
- Growth across US and international markets
- Food & beverage vertical: growth accelerated to 17% in Q3 (from 10% year-ago)
- New volume additions in food & beverage: jumped to 30% (from 2%)
- Focus areas: Food and beverage, larger sellers, international expansion

SQUARE AI:
Jack Dorsey wrote: "Square AI enables sellers to easily explore their data to uncover even more complex insights. Soon, it will proactively suggest actions to optimize growth or improve efficiency and implement them with a tap."

- Cash App Neighborhoods introduced to connect sellers with Cash App's 58M user network
- Square Bitcoin: integrated Bitcoin payments solution rolling out for sellers to accept Bitcoin and convert sales automatically
- Proto monetization plan: hardware sales across ASICs, mining hashboards, and full mining rigs
