=== CONTENT SUMMARY ===

PURPOSE / MISSION:
Block's ecosystems are united by a shared purpose of economic empowerment. The company builds technology to increase access to the global economy, helping individuals and businesses manage, move, and grow their money.

FINANCIAL PERFORMANCE — FY 2024:
- Total Net Revenue: $24,121 million (10% YoY increase from 2023)
- Gross Profit: $8,889 million (up from $7,505M in 2023, +18.4%)
- Operating Income: $892 million (vs. -$279M loss in 2023 — turnaround year)
- Net Income: $2,897 million (vs. $10M in 2023)
- EPS Basic: $4.70 (vs. $0.02 in 2023)
- EPS Diluted: $4.56

BUSINESS SEGMENTS:
- Square: Over 30 distinct products and services, 8+ geographies, 4+ million sellers, 5.2 billion transactions, $228 billion GPV in FY 2024
- Cash App: 57 million monthly transacting users in US; peer-to-peer payments, debit card, bill pay, lending
- TIDAL: Music streaming with 162+ million songs, 927,000 high-quality videos, 300+ label partnerships
- Bitcoin Ecosystem: Bitkey (self-custody wallet), Proto (mining system), Spiral (open-source contributions)

STRATEGIC PRIORITIES FOR FY 2024:
- Disciplined growth and cost efficiency initiatives
- AI technology integration across platforms
- Banking services expansion
- Vendor arrangement reassessment
- International market penetration

CAPITAL ALLOCATION:
- $2.0 billion senior unsecured notes issued (due 2032)
- $1.3 billion stock repurchase executed (under $4B authorization)
- $10.7 billion available liquidity ($9.9B in cash/equivalents/securities)
- $67.9 million committed to economic empowerment initiatives

WORKFORCE:
- Operating "below the employee cap through performance management"
- Function centralization underway

KEY RISK FACTORS:
- Cryptocurrency volatility exposure
- Third-party processing dependencies
- Data security vulnerabilities
- Regulatory compliance burdens
- Macroeconomic sensitivity
- AI regulation uncertainty

STRATEGIC CONTEXT:
FY 2024 represented Block's turnaround year — from operating loss to operating profit, from near-zero net income to $2.9B. This set the stage for the growth-with-profitability narrative that dominated 2025 strategy communications. The 10-K serves as the baseline for year-over-year drift analysis.
