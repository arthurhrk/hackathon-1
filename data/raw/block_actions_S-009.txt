BLOCK 8-K WORKFORCE REDUCTION FILING

FILING DETAILS:
- Filed: February 26, 2026
- Type: Form 8-K (Current Report — Material Event)
- Filer: Block, Inc. (NYSE: XYZ)

WORKFORCE REDUCTION PLAN:
- Block announced a workforce reduction restructuring plan to align organizational structure with operating model and strategic priorities
- Reduction: more than 40% of current workforce
- Pre-restructuring headcount: approximately 10,000+ employees
- Post-restructuring target: under 6,000 employees
- Approximately 4,000+ positions being eliminated

RESTRUCTURING CHARGES:
- Expected charges: $450 million to $500 million
- Primarily cash expenditures for employee severance and benefits
- Majority of charges to be incurred in Q1 2026
- Execution substantially complete by end of Q2 2026

STRATEGIC RATIONALE (per CEO Jack Dorsey):
- Described as "a choice about how we operate" the business
- Positioning Block as "a smaller, faster, intelligence-native company"
- AI-native restructuring: "Intelligence tools have changed what it means to build and run a company"
- Referenced Elon Musk's approach to organizational efficiency
- Stated belief that most companies will follow similar path

FINANCIAL CONTEXT:
- Announced simultaneously with Q4 2025 results showing strong performance
- Gross profit grew 24% YoY in Q4
- 2026 guidance raised alongside workforce cuts
- Stock initially fell but then soared as much as 24% following the announcement
- Morgan Stanley upgraded to Overweight following announcement

MARKET REACTION:
- Block shares surged as much as 24% following announcement
- Largest single-day gain in recent history
- Market interpreted cuts as credible path to dramatically higher margins
- Morgan Stanley raised price target to $93 with 2026 EPS projection of $3.81

HISTORICAL CONTEXT:
- This follows earlier layoffs: Block cut ~930 employees in March 2025
- Also follows November 2024 shutdown of TBD/Web5 and TIDAL scale-back
- Part of a multi-year efficiency push under Dorsey's leadership
