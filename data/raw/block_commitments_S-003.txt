BLOCK INVESTOR DAY 2025 PRESS RELEASE — COMMITMENTS

KEY COMMITMENTS IN PRESS RELEASE:

1. MULTI-YEAR OUTLOOK:
   Block outlined a three-year financial outlook through 2028 for senior leadership to present at Investor Day

2. $5 BILLION STOCK REPURCHASE INCREASE:
   Board approved a $5 billion increase to its existing share repurchase program for Class A common stock
   Total authorized: $9 billion
   Repurchases may be conducted through open market purchases or privately negotiated transactions
   Subject to market conditions and applicable legal requirements

3. 2026 SPECIFIC GUIDANCE (Investor Day version — later raised):
   Gross profit: $11.98 billion (+17% YoY)
   Adjusted operating income: $2.70 billion
   Adjusted EPS: $3.20

4. RULE OF 40 COMMITMENT:
   Expects to achieve Rule of 40 in 2026 and sustain through 2028
