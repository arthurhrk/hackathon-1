BLOCK Q1 2025 EARNINGS CALL — KEY ACTION EVIDENCE

FINANCIAL RESULTS (Q1 2025):
- Gross profit: $2.29 billion (+9% YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $813 million (+15% YoY)
- Adjusted operating income: $466 million (+28% YoY)
- Missed EPS and revenue expectations

KEY THEMES:
- Q1 represented a soft quarter with misses on headline EPS and revenue
- Management emphasized H2 2025 acceleration thesis
- Maintained full-year guidance despite Q1 softness
- Square market share gains narrative continued

CASH APP (Q1 2025):
- Cash App gross profit growth: ~10% YoY (slowest growth rate, pre-reacceleration)
- Cited seasonal patterns and prior-year comparison base
- Management committed to H2 growth acceleration driven by:
  - Cash App Afterpay integration
  - Expanded lending products
  - Cash App Card adoption growth

SQUARE (Q1 2025):
- Square gross profit growth: ~9% YoY
- GPV growth impacted by leap year headwind (~1 point) and FX headwind (~1 point)
- Adjusting for these, underlying growth was in high single digits
- Building toward low double-digit growth by year end

ORGANIZATIONAL CHANGES:
- March 2025: Block cut ~930 employees
- Described as reorganization step toward increasing company's stock value
- Preceded the larger 40%+ cut announced in February 2026

MANAGEMENT RESPONSE TO MISS:
- Defended full-year outlook
- Emphasized acceleration in H2 driven by product launches and seasonality
- Square growth expected to build from high single digits to low double digits
- Cash App growth expected to reaccelerate through Afterpay integration
