INVESTOR DAY OVERVIEW:
Block held its first Investor Day since 2022 on November 19, 2025. Senior leadership presented Block's strategy, product innovation, and financial outlook.

MULTI-YEAR FINANCIAL OUTLOOK (through 2028):

2026 Targets:
- Gross Profit: $11.98 billion (17% YoY growth)
- Adjusted Operating Income: $2.70 billion (growing over 30%)
- Adjusted EPS: $3.20
- Non-GAAP Cash Flow: 20% of gross profit, or $2.40 billion

2028 Targets:
- Gross Profit: approximately $15.8 billion
- Gross Profit growth: mid-teens range annually through 2028
- Adjusted Operating Income: $4.6 billion (growing about 30% annually)
- Adjusted Operating Income margin: 25% of gross profit by 2028
- Non-GAAP Cash Flow: more than $4.0 billion
- Adjusted EPS: $5.15
- Free Cash Flow: $4 billion

STRATEGIC FRAMEWORK:
- Rule of 40: Block expects to achieve Rule of 40 in 2026 and sustain through 2028
  (Rule of 40 = sum of gross profit growth + Adjusted Operating Income margin as % of gross profit)
- Operating leverage: Profitability compounding faster than gross profit growth
- Capital discipline: Profitable growth with operating leverage over three years

STOCK REPURCHASE PROGRAM:
- $5 billion increase to stock repurchase program
- Part of broader capital allocation strategy

SEGMENT PERFORMANCE CONTEXT:
- Block has doubled gross profit per active for Cash App since 2020
- Block has nearly doubled gross profit per seller for Square since 2020
- Cash App gross profit growth targets and Square GPV targets maintained through 2028
- Scale of ecosystems: Cash App, Square, and Proto

STRATEGIC THEMES:
- Profitable growth and operating leverage
- Scale of ecosystems (Cash App consumer network, Square seller network, Proto mining)
- Continued discipline in execution
- Three-year financial roadmap demonstrating maturation from growth to profitable growth
- S&P 500 inclusion (July 2025) as validation of maturation
