BLOCK Q4 2025 SHAREHOLDER LETTER — FORWARD-LOOKING COMMITMENTS

CEO JACK DORSEY COMMITMENTS:

1. AI-NATIVE OPERATING MODEL:
   "Intelligence tools have changed what it means to build and run a company. A significantly smaller team, using the tools we're building, can do more and do it better."
   "Intelligence tool capabilities are compounding faster every week."

2. WORKFORCE TRANSFORMATION:
   "I don't think we're early to this realization. I think most companies are late."
   "Within the next year, I believe the majority of companies will reach the same conclusion and make similar structural changes."
   Commitment to "flatten organizational structure, clarify accountability, and concentrate resources on highest-priority initiatives"

3. STRATEGIC FOCUS AREAS:
   Identified highest-priority initiatives: Square, Cash App, and broader ecosystem
   Commitment to maintaining Bitkey (self-custody bitcoin wallet) and Proto (bitcoin mining) investments
