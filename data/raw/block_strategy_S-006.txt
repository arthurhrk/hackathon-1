=== CONTENT SUMMARY ===

MISSION AND PURPOSE:
Block, Inc. is building an ecosystem dedicated to economic empowerment and unlocking access to the financial system for people around the world. The company's purpose is economic empowerment — helping individuals and businesses manage, move, and grow their money through simple and connected tools. Mission statement: "To empower people with economic tools to access and thrive in the economy."

Block builds technology to increase access to the global economy. The company's ecosystems are united by a shared purpose of economic empowerment, with each ecosystem serving different people — sellers, consumers, artists, fans, and developers.

BUSINESS DESCRIPTION:
Block designs and operates connected ecosystems that integrate commerce solutions, financial services, software, hardware, and networks to serve individuals and small businesses, primarily through Cash App's consumer network and Square's business ("seller") network. The platform integrates payments, banking, lending, and commerce solutions designed to provide secure, reliable, and scalable financial infrastructure.
